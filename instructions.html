Welcome to Read-Easy

Our website aims to enhance the usability and readability of text for everyone. Whether you're a student, a professional, or just someone who loves reading, our tools are here to make your reading experience better.

Features

  • Text-to-Speech: Easily listen to your text with our built-in voice reader.
  • Customizable Display: Adjust font size, style, and colors to suit your preferences.

We believe that reading should be accessible to all, and our tools are designed to help achieve that goal. Dive in and explore!
